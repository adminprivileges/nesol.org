Transitioning from the military? NE Solutions has internship opportunities to assist service members transition into civilian life as part of a highly skilled and fast paced team. Skillbridge Internships are available for any of our open positions on the Careers page. If any of the positions fit your interests and qualifications, please contact us using the form below to begin the application process. 

### Want to join our team? [Contact Us](/contact)

Through the Department of Defense (DoD) SkillBridge Program, NE Solutions will employ active-duty Service Members for training for up to the final six months of your active-duty service obligation. Any rank, enlisted or officer, may apply. Your Commander must authorize participation in NE Solution's SkillBridge Program before starting your internship.

Interns are not eligible for monetary compensation through NE Solutions for the duration of their internship as they will still be paid through their service elements. Upon completion of the skillbridge program, eligible interns will interview for one of our Careers. Please consult the Dod Skillbridge program site for further information: https://skillbridge.osd.mil/

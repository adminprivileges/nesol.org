NE Solutions is currently seeking Instructors to deliver training as part of the new Network Analyst training course. Network Analyst Instructors will be part of a collaborative multidisciplinary Team delivering highly sophisticated, technical, hands-on training solutions to meet the mission and time-critical needs of our customers. 

Network Analyst Instructor candidates provide classroom instruction, provide lab experience, and evaluate students on the knowledge required to meet the certification standards for their career path. In addition:
- The instructor works with a multidisciplinary Team of professionals to develop and update course content, training materials, and documentation. 
- The Instructors work with the students to prepare them to meet the mission-critical needs of our customers
- The Instructors monitor student performance on practical exercises and provide feedback to students
- Virtualization technology is employed heavily to simulate real-world scenarios that create an immersive learning environment for students

## Requirements
- Clearance: TS/SCI w/Polygraph
- Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science (or related field) is desired, but not required 
- Minimum Years of Experience: 
  - Level 1: Minimum two (2) years of experience in the development, testing, deployment, operations, training, or maintenance of electrical/electronic/software systems pertaining to Computer Network Operations.  An additional three (3) years of experience may be substituted for a Bachelor’s degree for a total of five (5) years of experience.
  - Level 2: Minimum five (5) years of experience in the development, testing, deployment, operations, training, or maintenance of electrical/electronic/software systems pertaining to Computer Network Operations.  An additional three (3) years of experience may be substituted for a Bachelor’s degree for a total of eight (8) years of experience.
- Experience in supporting CNO operations support training for Cyber Navigation and Tactical Forensics Analysis is highly desired 
- Administration knowledge of setting up and maintaining common Windows and/or Unix variant servers
- Demonstrate knowledge of creating and configuring virtual hosts and networks using VMware in support of developing and/or instructing realistic scenarios
- Demonstrate knowledge of commonly used software-programming languages (to include, but not limited to: Python, Ruby, and/or shell scripting).
- Experience creating and/or using training materials utilizing tools such as MOODLE, Captivate, Camtasia, Articulate, or other course and learning management systems (LMS)

## Preferred
- The Network Analyst Instructor positions requires fully cleared individuals with an extensive range of experience, including the following:
  - Conduct research and evaluate technical and all-source intelligence with specific emphasis on network operations and cyber warfare tactics, techniques, and procedures focused on the threat to networked weapons platforms and US and DoD information networks
  - Analyze network events to determine the impact on current operations and conduct all-source research to determine advisory capability and intent
  - Prepare assessments and cyber threat profiles of current events based on the sophisticated collection, research and analysis of classified and open source information
  - Correlate threat data from various sources
  - Develop and maintain analytical procedures to meet changing requirements and ensure maximum operations
  - Collect data using a combination of standard intelligence methods and business processes.
  - Produce high-quality papers, presentations, recommendations, and findings for senior US government intelligence and network operations officials
  - Provide recommendations for technical solutions to a wide range of difficult problems
  - Under supervision, draft and recommend approach to solutions
  - General Windows and Unix knowledge
  - Experience as a trainer or course developer for cyber courses
  - Effective communication, writing, documentation, and requirements gatherings skills are needed to be effective in this role for working with other members of the Team and with the Customer to meet the requirements of the program
  - Provide recommendations for technical solutions to a wide range of difficult problems
  - Provides mentoring to junior Team Members

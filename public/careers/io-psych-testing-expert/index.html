IO Psych Testing Expert

NE Solutions is seeking an experienced Instructional Testing Expert to support our federal customer in Hanover, MD (Remote). This is a temporary assignment scheduled to last 3-4 months.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and revise tests and/or assessments for highly technical evaluation programs
  • Work closely with content developers and subject matter experts (SMEs) to design and develop formative and summative assessments
  • Conduct data gathering and needs analyses to ensure learning objectives; Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities (KSA); and/or Job Qualification Standards (JQS) are mapped to test questions and case studies
  • Develop test blueprints and oversee the creation of job-knowledge tests and performance-oriented assessments
  • Develop reports to satisfy contractual reporting requirements
  • Identify and develop the assessment and/or testing methods for learning solutions
  • Develop the testing strategies for the program
  • Apply validity and reliability standards to ensure tests are psychometrically sound
  • Revise tests and/or assessments based on beta and pilot offerings of learning programs
  • Write contract documentation to support all aspects of testing on the program
  • Conduct research and provide recommendations on processes and procedures for test creation and administration
  • Collaborate with subject matter experts and multi-disciplinary team members to develop measurable learning objectives and integrate tests into the course content of learning program
  • Develop and utilize paper or electronic assessment instruments, such as multiple-choice selected responses, open ended, essay, case studies, performance-based tasks, role-play, pre-and post-tests, and simulations
  • Conduct standard setting activities
  • Apply psychometric formulas and theory to construct valid and reliable tests and measures of knowledge and/or performance-based assessments
  • Conduct exam and item analysis
  • Maintain detailed records of the exams for the program

Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Experience designing, developing, and revising tests and/or assessments for highly technical evaluation programs
  • Possess a broad range of experience related to test design and exam item analysis methodologies to effectively support customers with rapidly evolving requirements
  • Familiarity with tools such as Excel, SPSS Statistics software, and applications used to deliver testing solutions
  • Effective communication, writing, and requirements gatherings skills
  • US Citizenship is required
  • Time Management Skills — Managing one’s own time and the time of others. The ability to cooperate and collaborate with team members to adhere to project schedules and tight deadlines without sacrificing quality

Preferred:

  • Academic/training industry experience
  • Familiarity with DOD terminology
  • Ph.D. in Industrial/Organizational Psychology or Instructional Design and Human Performance Improvement (or similar degree)
